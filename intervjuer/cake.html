Vad skriver du?
Cake

Var fick du ditt namn ifrån?
I had  a couple of tags before i settled for this one. One of the writers

Hur länge har du varit i denna branchen?
This all depends on how you see it. I got interessed when i was about 12 or 13 this was aound 1990 - 1991. I did alot of sketching and fooled around with it. In 1994 i met Newer (He had a totaly different tag back then). At this time he went in 7:th grade. We started to hang ot and paint. We became the local toys and started to paint more and more. We hooked up with 2 other painters and started to paint with them also. But sadly they quit the aerosoul business after about 1 year.

Vilka / vilket crew representerar du?
I represent MSA, SOS, MG

Vad tycker du om graffiti scenen i helsingborg? motivera dig,
I think that its a pale shadow of what it once was. Now people are just out for the fame, and are trying to be cool. Everybody is just calling themselfs kings. Its truly sad to see people who absolutele have no knowing of the Helsinborg Graffiti history go around bragging about this and that. They should look up their roots and try to open their eyes...

Ser du dig själv som en vandal eller artist?
I look upon myself as writer, whatever that meens is decided by the culture.

Vem / var fick du inspiration ifrån?
I got my inspiration from SER, SKEY & SERPENT. The two first writers where much older than me when i started. But SERPENT was approx my own age. Nowadays i get inspiration from all kinds of thing around me. But a big inspiration is my good friend Newer.

Vad tycker du om alla toysen?
Good , but they need to do the same that we did when we started. Go out and check tags and look up spots, Try to find pieces. Take lots of photos. And stay home and sketch alot, it helps a great deal. But ofcourse times have changed since i started.... so....

Vilka burkar andvänder du dig av?
Quick, Belton, Biltema, Guthaus, Auto-K, Spraila, Whatever i can get my hands on....

Vad är det bästa med att måla?
Its a great culture to be involved in, to see yourself and your friends develop.
